Frequently Asked Questions


1HOW TO REGISTER AN ACCOUNT?

Follow the steps below:

  1. Click on "Register" in Homepage or on the top right corner
  2. Enter the registration details on the form
  3. After entering the details, click "Register".
  4. Now, you can login and view/register for events and workshops
2How to register for a event?

Follow the steps below

  1. Login into your account and Click "Events" in the sidebar
  2. Select your department
  3. If it is Individual Event then click Add to cart and your registration will be added to cart.
  4. If it is Team Event then click Add team to cart
  5. Enter the team name and type the name of the all the members
  6. Only the team leader must add the members during the registration of the event, all the members will be registered automatically.
  7. You will receive a notification on the top of the screen if the registration is successful
3How to register for a workshop?

Follow the steps below

  1. Login into your account and Click "workshop" in the sidebar
  2. Select your department and clickAdd to cart
  3. You will receive a notification on the top of the screen if the registration is successful
4HOW TO MAKE PAYMENT?

Follow the steps below

  1. Login into your account and Click "Cart" in the sidebar
  2. Select Confirm Carton the top right corner
    YOU CANNOT REGISTER ANY EVENTS/WORKSHOPS AFTER CONFIRMING CART
  3. You will receive a notification that your cart is confirmed. Now select Online Payment
  4. Online Payment :Click Pay and you will be taken to the online payment portal
5HOW TO APPLY FOR ACCOMMODATION?

Follow the steps below

  1. Login into your account and Click "Accomodation" in the sidebar
  2. Select the number of days of stay
  3. Once the admin approves your accommodation request, you can pay through online payment
6 I couldn't login.

Click " Forget password" and you will receive the steps to reset the password to your registered mail

8 What is the fee for event and workshops?

  • Rs.250 (excluding Early-bird offer) irrespective of number of events
  • The payment for the workshop is listed in the details of workshop
9 I Couldn't add a member to my team

It may be one of the reasons below

  • Team max limit reached

10 Should every member for the team register for the event seperately?

No, Only the team leader must add the members during the registration of the event, all the members will be registered automatically.

11 Can I withdraw from an event/workshop?

Yes, If you haven't paid for the event/workshop, you can withdraw from the event/workshop you want to from the cart section.
